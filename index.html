About Us

Soldiers of Christ is a mentorship group led by Prophetess Jessica Harrison. A community of incredible ladies and gents, this group focuses on building a real relationship with Jesus, knowing your purpose, and walking in it.

Our Activities

  • 📖 Bible Study
  • 🙏 Prayer Meetings
  • 🤝 One-on-one Mentorship

Meet The Mentor

Prophetess Jessica Harrison is joyfully married to her beloved husband, Prophet GD Harrison, and together they are blessed with two beautiful daughters. Currently residing in Canada, she is a woman of purpose, passion, and vision. With a heart deeply rooted in God’s calling, Prophetess Jessica is devoted to empowering women both single and married to discover and walk boldly in their God-given purpose. Her ministry is centered on nurturing purposeful living, building strong families, and raising women who shine with grace, strength, and spiritual insight. She is committed to seeing women transformed by the power of God, equipped to thrive in every season of life, and positioned to impact their world for Christ.
